About

DSAtlas is an interactive map of Democratic Socialists of America chapter membership across the United States. Hover and click on the map to discover more information about local chapters and membership.

Data

  • Individual chapter metrics are voluntarily reported.
  • State, county and congressional district boundaries provided by the United States Census
  • Map tiles provided by the OpenStreetMap Project
